Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

PocketOracle ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when you use the PocketOracle mobile application ("the App") and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information We Do Not Collect

PocketOracle does not collect, store, or transmit your questions or any personally identifiable information to our servers. All oracle answers are generated locally on your device from a built-in set of responses — no question text or personal content ever leaves your device.

1.2 Information Collected by Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, which may collect data as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google) – collects anonymous usage events to help us understand how the App is used. Events include: screen views, questions asked, language selected, edition switched (Love / Standard), upgrade button tapped, and dialogs shown or dismissed. No personal or identifying information is attached to these events. See the Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Remote Config (Google) – used to remotely configure app behavior such as the daily question limit. See the Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat – used to manage in-app subscriptions and purchase status. RevenueCat may collect purchase history and subscription state tied to your App Store or Play Store account. See the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

2. Local Data Storage

PocketOracle stores the following data locally on your device using secure on-device storage. This data never leaves your device:

  • Your selected app language
  • Whether Love Edition mode is enabled
  • Your daily question usage count (used to enforce the free-tier limit)

3. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Subscription and one-time purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not have access to your payment information. Purchase and subscription status is managed via RevenueCat as described in Section 1.2.

4. How We Use Information

Anonymous analytics data is used solely to improve the App — for example, understanding which features are used and whether daily limits are appropriately set. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties beyond what is described in this policy.

5. Data Retention

Local on-device data is retained until you delete the App. Analytics data retained by Firebase is subject to Google's data retention policies. RevenueCat retains purchase data in accordance with their privacy policy.

6. Children's Privacy

PocketOracle is not specifically directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data, including the right to access, correct, or delete information held about you. Since we do not directly store personal data, requests should be directed to the relevant third-party services (Google/Firebase, RevenueCat) or to your device's app settings to clear local data. For any other inquiries, contact us at the address below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
