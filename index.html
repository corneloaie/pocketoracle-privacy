Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

PocketOracle ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when you use the PocketOracle mobile application and how it is used.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information. However, the following third-party services integrated into the app may collect data as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) – used for remote configuration and app functionality. See the Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat – used to manage in-app purchases and subscription status. See the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

Data collected by third-party SDKs is used solely to deliver app functionality — including remote configuration and purchase management. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with additional third parties beyond what those integrated services require to operate.

Data Storage

PocketOracle stores minimal data locally on your device (such as app preferences and language settings) using secure on-device storage. This data does not leave your device.

In-App Purchases

Subscription and purchase transactions are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store) and managed via RevenueCat. We do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

PocketOracle is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
